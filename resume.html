More About Dr. Aliyu Idris Funtua

Summary

Dr Aliyu Idris Funtua

Dr Aliyu Idris Funtua is a Nigerian academic and the Provost of Federal College of Education, Katsina.
He became the 13th Provost of the College on August, 19, 2016.

Primary and Secondary Education

Primary School Education

Year: 1970

School: Aya Primary School, Funtua

The 1970 intake into Aya Primary School, Funtua comprised of over fourty Students drawn within Funtua and its environs. A few of Dr Aliyu Idris Funtuas’ classmate are Business men, traditional rulers and season’s civil servants. This include Prof. Idris Isa Funtua (Former Vice Chancellor to Umar Musa Yar’adua University, Katsina), Yusuf Abba a business man residing in Katsina, Alhaji Bature Abdulkadir who is the Danejin Katsina, District head of Mahuta, Alhaji Bello Muhammad the present Director of Agriculture, Katsina State Ministry of Agriculture, the Galadiman Funtua Alhaji Kabir Idris Sambo. Others are Alhaji Sani Umar, Malam Muazu Bello,
Malam Ibrahim Shittu, Bature Abdulkadir, Yusuf Musa, among others.

Secondary School Education

Year: 1976

School: Government Secondary School Malumfashi

On successful completion of Primary School in Funtua, Aliyu move to Government Secondary School Malumfashi in 1976 which he triumphantly completed in 1981.
On arrival to Malumfashi because of his friendly nature he was able to have friends some from his own class, while other from other class although the same mate. Among his early friends were, Dr. Mustapha Abubakar Malumfashi (Head of Department Hausa F.C.E. Katsina), Alh. Bello Buhari (Deputy Bursar F.C.E. Katsina), Alhaji Mustapha Gege (Custom Controller) Umaru Sani Masanawa, Bishir Isa among others.

In his Secondary School days he held many post such as prefect (that is House Captain, of Musa House), member Muslim Students Society (M.S.S.) among others. All these have significantly started to indicate the good leadership style of Sadauki.

Tertiary Education

Tertiary Education and Working Experience

Year: 1981
Institution: Advance Teachers College Katsina, which is now called Federal College of Education

After his Secondary School education, he got admission into the then Advance Teachers College Katsina, which is now called Federal College of Education, Katsina in 1981 and successfully completed in 1984 and obtained Nigeria Certificate in Education (N.C.E.). Picture of Dr Aliyu Provost at F.C.E. Katsina at his N.C.E. Program and group picture together with class mate and their Lecturer (Prof. Manir Mamman ) at N.C.E level.

At that time, N.C.E. graduates use to go for National Youth Service Corps (N.Y.S.C). Hence he was posted to Borno State and did his Primary assignment at Government Secondary School Gwoza between 1984 – 1985. On successful completion of N.Y.S.C., he took an appointment with the then Kaduna State Government under Ministry of Education where he was posted as classroom Teacher as Master Grade III. In 1985, He rolls from Master Grade III up to post of Vice Principal in 1997.

For any teacher to be functional, active and update, he has to update his knowledge from term to term. It is in view of this Dr. Aliyu applied to Bayero University Kano (B.U.K.), and were admitted to read B.A.Ed. Hausa in 1990 and completed in 1994. After the programme he came back to his work and function effectively as classroom Teacher.

His selfless service, hard work, dedication and good teaching abilities among others assisted him in getting appointment with his alma-mata College, which is Federal College of Education, Katsina. He was employed as lecturer II / HATISS 09 in 1997 and posted to Hausa Department.

However to fit into the world of globalization the Sadauki enrol for Computer Certificate in the College (F.C.E. Katsina) in 2003 and graduated successfully as Computer literate lecturer.

The former, he returned to his alma-mate University (B.U.K.) to commence his master’s degree in 2001 and completed in 2004. He wrote his dissertation on the "Wakokin Siyasa a Jamhoriya ta Ukku, Yanayinsu da siffufinsu". At the compilation of his master’s programme in 2004, he resumed work in earnest at the Federal College of Education Katsina. It is this master’s degree that marks him out as a well-versed and intellectually forceful, as equipped with enough knowledge (content), methodology as well as necessary skills to deliver/teach or lecture students. During this period exemplary, visionary and potential leadership began not only to manifest but to become vividly clear, that he is going to be a great leader. This was noticed by the then Management of the College, and began to give him series of appointments, where he served successfully.

In 2011 Sadauki Dr Aliyu didn’t relent in his effort to further his education. In view of this he went back to B.U.K. for his Ph.D. programme. He successfully completed in 2016 with conferment of Doctor of Philosophy in Hausa, by the Senate of Bayero University Kano.

As mentioned earlier, the young Dr Aliyu after spending thirteen years (13) teaching at secondary school he transfer his service to Federal College of Education Katsina (F.C.E.KT.) where he was appointed as lecturer II / HATISS 09 in 1997. This appointment was successful due to high level of commitment to his teaching career. In the year 2000, with satisfactory service at F.C.E. Katsina was promoted to rank of lecturer I. Three years later he became Senior Lecturer in 2003 after fulfilling all necessary conditions and successful service.

    Aliyu’s catopulties to lime light began when in 2003 he was promoted to the rank of Principal Lecturer. It was the much-heralded moment, when he reached the apex of his academic career (In tertiary institution) with his promotion to the rank of Chief Lecturer in 2011.
