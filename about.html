ABOUT

Dr. Aliyu Idris Funtua FIICA, FICA, FCPS, MNAE

The Provost, Federal College of Education Katsina

Dr Aliyu Idris Funtua is a Nigerian academic and the Provost of Federal College of Education, Katsina.

  • Birthday: 1960
  • City: Katsina Nigeria
  • Age: 64
  • Degree: Phd

Born on Friday, 12th April, 1960 brought an unparalleled blessing of a baby boy to the family of Alhaji Idris Maikaji and Hajiya Hassana at Dandume in then Funtua Local Government area of Katsina State, seven days later, he was named in consonance with the Islamic precepts Aliyu.

He grew in an extended family amidst innumerable kinsmen in Funtua. Aliyu educational sojourn began in Islamic School before he went to Aya Primary School Funtua in 1970, where he finished and obtained Primary School leaving certificate in 1976.
